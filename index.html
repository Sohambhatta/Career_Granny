About Career Granny

Our Mission

Career Granny started as a free AI tech career counselor tool and has evolved into a comprehensive 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We're dedicated to bridging the gap between students and successful tech careers through personalized guidance, skill assessments, and educational resources.

What We Offer

  • AI-powered career counseling for hundreds of tech careers
  • Skill assessment and best-fit career matching
  • Free educational resources and guides
  • Workshops and networking events
  • Partnerships with schools and colleges
